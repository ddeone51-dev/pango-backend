Privacy Policy for Pango

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pango ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") to find and book accommodations in Tanzania.

By using Pango, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

  • Name: First and last name
  • Email Address: For account management and communication
  • Phone Number: For account verification and booking confirmations
  • Password: Encrypted and securely stored
  • Profile Photo: Optional, if you choose to upload one

2.2 Location Information

We collect location data to provide you with:

  • Nearby accommodation listings
  • Property locations on interactive maps
  • Location-based search results
  • Distance calculations

Location data is collected only when you use map features or search for accommodations. You can disable location access in your device settings, but some features may not work properly.

2.3 Booking Information

  • Booking dates and details
  • Number of guests
  • Special requests or preferences
  • Booking history

2.4 Payment Information

Payment information is processed securely through our payment partner, Pesapal. We do not store your complete credit card numbers or sensitive payment data on our servers. We only store transaction IDs and payment status.

2.5 Host Information

If you list properties as a host, we collect:

  • Property details (title, description, amenities)
  • Property location and address
  • Pricing information
  • Property photos
  • Availability calendar

2.6 Usage Information

  • App features you use
  • Search queries
  • Properties you view or save
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App crashes and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

3.1 Providing Services

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing and confirming bookings
  • Facilitating communication between guests and hosts
  • Processing payments securely
  • Displaying properties on maps
  • Showing personalized search results

3.2 Improving Our Services

  • Understanding how users interact with the app
  • Identifying and fixing bugs
  • Developing new features
  • Improving search and recommendation algorithms

3.3 Communication

  • Sending booking confirmations
  • Sending payment receipts
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Sending important app updates (with your consent)

3.4 Safety and Security

  • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • Enforcing our terms and policies
  • Protecting user safety
  • Resolving disputes

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

4.1 With Hosts and Guests

When you book a property, we share necessary information (name, contact details, booking dates) with the host. Similarly, hosts' contact information is shared with confirmed guests.

4.2 With Service Providers

  • Google Maps: For map and location services
  • Pesapal: For secure payment processing
  • Firebase: For authentication and app analytics
  • Cloud Hosting: For data storage (MongoDB Atlas)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/SSL
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on secure cloud servers
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security practices reviewed regularly

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Pango is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide services
  • Required by law
  • Necessary for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Tanzania. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

10. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences (language, saved searches)
  • Understand app usage patterns

You can manage these through your device settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification (for major changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
